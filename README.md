# Neff Co — AI Agent Organization
## Executive Brief · May 26, 2026

---

## The Structure

Neff Co runs on a 16+ agent hierarchy across three cost tiers. Every agent has a defined role, a model matched to the job, and a clear reporting line. No redundancy. No waste.

---

## Leadership

**Axel Neff** · CEO & Founder
The human in the loop. All strategic decisions, approvals, and direction flow from Axel. Every agent ultimately serves his priorities.

**Fritz** · President / CPO · *Claude Opus 4.6*
Axel's right hand. Strategy, coordination, knowledge work, and Axel's voice when needed. Fritz doesn't write code or do bookkeeping. He thinks, decides, and delegates. Reports directly to Axel.

---

## Fritz's Direct Reports

**Richard Hendricks (Dick)** · CTO · *Claude Sonnet 4*
Owns all engineering. Receives specs from Fritz, breaks them into tasks, distributes to engineers, reviews output through Codex. Architects systems, rewrites bad code, maintains quality standards. If it touches a codebase, Dick owns it.

**Sophie** · Program Manager / PMO · *Gemini Flash-Lite*
The operational nervous system. Reads Trello and Linear, produces status briefs, tracks tasks, flags missed handoffs, manages the credential index. Runs on deterministic code for 90% of her work. Costs less than $0.50/month. Fritz and Axel never read Trello directly. Sophie reads it and tells them what matters.

**Kimi** · VP Operations · *Kimi K2.5/K2.6 (Allegretto)*
Deep research, TH operations analysis, email triage, real estate due diligence. Kimi is remote (Singapore servers), incredibly powerful for long-context work (256K window), but isolated. Fritz acts as the bridge, providing browser and tool access that Kimi lacks. Free with Allegretto subscription.

**ChatGPT** · Finance · *GPT-5.5*
Tax strategy, ROBS structure, Neff Global Co. entity management. Axel drives this directly. ChatGPT has context on the financial history that other agents don't. Stays in its lane.

## Cost Tiers

| Tier | Agents | Monthly Est. |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Premium** (Opus/Sonnet) | Fritz, Dick | ~$200-400 (capped at $10/day) |
| **Mid** (Gemini Flash, GPT) | Sophie, Lena, Codex, Gemini, Gilfoyle, Dinesh | ~$10-30 |
| **Free** (Gemma 4, Kimi, Ollama, Grok) | Franz + 6 engineers, Forge, Kimi, Ollama | $0 |

**Design principle:** Premium models for judgment. Mid-tier for structured work. Free for volume. 80% of engineering runs at $0.

---

## How It Works in Practice

1. **Axel** says "fix the fake counter on zen.net"
2. **Fritz** scopes the work, writes the spec
3. **Sophie** creates the Trello card + Linear tickets, assigns owners
4. **Dick** breaks it into engineer tasks
5. **Franz's team** (Atlas + Nova) implements on Gemma 4 26B ($0)
6. **Codex** reviews the code
7. **Forge** deploys to Render
8. **Sophie** updates the board, reports completion

Total cost for that workflow: ~$0.03 in Fritz's thinking time. Engineering: $0.
